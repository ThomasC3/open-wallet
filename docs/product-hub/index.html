Strategic Overview

Tokenized wallets that compound revenue and loyalty.

Open Wallet pre-funds user accounts, routes transactions through an internal tokenized ledger, and reduces exposure to payment processors that add 2‑3% per swipe. These design choices map to the data findings below.

  • Wallet adoption: FIS reports digital wallets already account for half of global ecommerce and are on track for 61% by 2027, so merchant experiences must assume wallet primacy.
  • Stored-value float: MarketWatch notes Starbucks holds over $1.8B in customer deposits, showing how closed-loop wallets finance working capital.
  • Compliance leverage: Basis Theory’s PCI scope analysis shows merchants can offload up to 93% of PCI controls by vaulting sensitive data in a tokenized store.
